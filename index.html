Long before the videos on the DHMK Youtube channel, our heroes and villians were much different. The Goofy Ball Stealer was a small child, The Straightinator was still your average Gym Kingdom castle guard, and it was overall happier times. But when the King left on a journey to find the lost queen, his son caused unfixable damages.
